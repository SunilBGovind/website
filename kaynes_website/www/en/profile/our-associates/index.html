Our Associates

Technology Partners

Our services and products are strengthened by alliances with leading technology partners. Our network of alliance and teaming relationships creates business value, reduces risk and accelerates go-to-market.

We work with our partners to increase the product value, lower costs, expand markets and geographic reach and enhance product and service offerings.

Kaynes along with its associates jointly deliver solutions that address our clients’ business and product life cycle. Globally India commands a huge market which is complex, price sensitive and value driven and not an easy one to penetrate. We understand that most of the overseas product are overbuild for this complex market and hence work with our associates to customize features to lower price and make the customer’s product more competitive.

We have partnered with our associate to address the growing Point of Sales (POS) and Unique Identification Market (UID). We are  independent hardware vendor for one leading MNC and our team works with the customer to bring out products suitable for the Indian market. Similarly we work with overseas Design companies in the Defence & Aerospace domain to bring our products for the Indian Offset market.

Electronic Packaging & Integrated Solutions Partners:

Sourcing Partners

Supply chain constraints in India is a major challenge for the Indian EMS companies. Kaynes has supply chain partners in North America and Singapore who are able to source Military Grade and hard to locate components, consolidate, kit and ship them JIT (Just In Time), thereby reducing inventory.

Kaynes also works closely with Distributors like Avnet, Arrow, Future and others on a three monthly rolling forecast. The system of Bonded Inventory (BIM) Model is agreed with these distributers on an annual basis resulting in win-win situation for all of the stake holders.

Warehousing Partners

Kaynes for its overseas customer follows a Vendor Managed Inventory programme, where the customer based on criticality of PCBAs or Products gives a Minimum order quantity to be placed at either his place or at warehouse selected by Kaynes. This order is an open one and as and when customer pulls the material, Kaynes gets paid. This benefits the customer immensely as it allows safety net and ensures customers line will not stop.

Kaynes also ships stock monthly to the warehouses and from there the exact quantity required by the customer is shipped based on his requirement.
